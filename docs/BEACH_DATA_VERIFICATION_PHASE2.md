# Beach Data Verification Phase 2

Phase 2 is the source-backed audit pass for launch trust. The goal is not to make every beach look complete; it is to remove false certainty and make every important claim traceable.

## Scope

- Access: asphalt road, passable dirt road, difficult dirt road, hiking path, boat-only.
- Amenities: sunbeds, umbrellas, showers, WC, changing rooms, snack bar, beach bar, tavernas, parking.
- Safety and suitability signals: lifeguard, family-friendly, snorkeling, quiet, nudism, accessibility.
- Confidence: `high` and `medium` should have evidence, not just plausible local knowledge.

## Verification Rules

- Direct `sourceUrls` are required for lifeguard claims.
- Direct `sourceUrls` are required for hard access claims: `difficult_dirt_road`, `hiking_path_difficult`, `boat_only`.
- Beach bar and organized-service claims should have a direct source URL or be downgraded.
- If sources conflict, keep the more conservative tourist-facing wording.
- Unknown is better than false certainty.
- "Phase 2 audit" notes are not counted as evidence by the coverage script. A source URL or pre-existing verification source is preferred.
