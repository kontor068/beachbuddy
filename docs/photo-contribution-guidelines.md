# Photo Contribution Guidelines

Use this guide before adding any submitted or manually sourced beach photo to the app.

## Accepted Sources

- Own photos submitted by the photographer.
- Wikimedia Commons files with clear license metadata.
- CC0 or Public Domain images.
- CC BY images.
- CC BY-SA images.

## Rejected Sources

- Google Images.
- Travel blogs or tourism articles without explicit reusable license terms.
- Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, TikTok, or other social posts without direct written permission.
- TripAdvisor, Booking, Airbnb, hotel listings, or restaurant listings.
- GNTO / Visit Greece Image Bank for now.
- Paid stock websites or paid licenses.
- Editorial-only images.
- Non-commercial licenses such as CC BY-NC or CC BY-NC-SA.
- Unknown author, unknown license, unclear source page, or unclear permission.

## Required Metadata

Every approved photo entry must include:

- Beach name and beach id.
- Image URL or submitted file reference.
- Thumbnail URL or app-safe optimized derivative.
- Author / photographer.
- License name.
- License URL.
- Source page URL.
- Attribution text.
- Permission notes when the image comes from a direct manual permission or own-photo submission.

## Review Rule

Never publish a photo until it has been manually verified for:

- Correct beach match.
- Clear commercial/app-compatible usage rights.
- Complete attribution metadata.
- Safe thumbnail/card URL that does not load an oversized original image.

Candidate, fallback, or incomplete entries must remain hidden from the automatic UI.
